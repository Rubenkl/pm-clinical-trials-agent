🎯 Strategic Options: Build vs Buy vs Partner

🏗️ Build (Current Approach)

Pros:

  • Complete control and IP ownership
  • Custom clinical trials optimization
  • Direct customer relationships
  • Higher long-term margins

Cons:

  • Longer time to market
  • Higher development investment
  • Technical execution risk

💰 Buy (Acquisition)

Pros:

  • Instant market presence
  • Existing customer base
  • Proven technology
  • Experienced team

Cons:

  • High acquisition cost ($100M+)
  • Integration complexity
  • Cultural misalignment risk
  • Technology debt

🤝 Partner (License/Joint Venture)

Pros:

  • Shared development costs
  • Combined expertise
  • Faster market entry
  • Risk sharing

Cons:

  • Shared IP and profits
  • Less control
  • Partner dependency
  • Alignment challenges

🎯 Recommended: Hybrid Build + Strategic Partnerships

Build core AI orchestration platform while partnering for:

  • 🔗 Integrations: Partner with EDC/CTMS vendors
  • 🚀 Distribution: CRO channel partnerships
  • 🤖 Technology: OpenAI Agents SDK alliance
  • 🏢 Enterprise: System integrator partnerships
